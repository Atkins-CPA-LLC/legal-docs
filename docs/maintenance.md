# Legal Document Maintenance

This repository stores shared legal document drafts for Atkins CPA applications.
It currently contains:

- `eula.md` - End-User License Agreement for the Atkins CPA Firm AI Financial Analyst application.
- `privacy-policy.md` - Privacy policy for the Atkins CPA Firm AI Financial Analyst application.

## Review Checklist

Before publishing or linking these documents from an application, review:

- Application name and covered product scope.
- Last-updated dates in every document.
- Company name, mailing address, and contact email.
- QuickBooks Online, Supabase, OpenRouter, and AI provider references.
- Data collection, storage, retention, and deletion language.
- User rights and support contact instructions.

## Change Policy

Keep updates documentation-only in this repository. If an application changes its
authentication, data storage, AI provider usage, retention rules, or user rights,
update the matching legal document and request legal review before relying on the
new text in production.
